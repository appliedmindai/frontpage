Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Applied Mind Company (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services, including our iOS applications, web platforms, and data-centric tools.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, secure, and improve our services. This includes:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and organization name.
  • User Content: Files, documents, and other structured content you explicitly upload.
  • Communications: Feedback, survey responses, and support requests.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Actions taken within the app, pages visited, features used, and time spent.
  • Device & Log Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers (e.g., IDFA or Android ID where permitted), and crash logs.
  • Metadata: Technical data related to the files you upload (e.g., file size, timestamps, format).

C. System Permissions (Mobile Apps)

Depending on your device settings, our AI applications may request access to:

  • Camera/Photos: This allows the AI to see what you see and provide relevant assistance or context.
  • Microphone: To allow the AI to listen to your voice commands and natural language requests.
  • Notifications: To send timely updates.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and authenticate your access to our tools.
  • R&D and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our algorithms, machine learning models, and user experience. (Note: We may use aggregated, de-identified data for these purposes).
  • Communication: To send administrative updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent activity, ensure compliance with our terms, and protect our platform.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted vendors (e.g., cloud infrastructure, analytics, customer support) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or valid legal process, or to protect the safety and rights of Applied Mind, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security and Retention

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the requested services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.
  • Enforce our agreements.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal data.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and associated data. To request deletion, please contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" feature within the application settings.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.

Applied Mind does not sell your personal information. To formally record a "Do Not Sell" request, email us at privacy@appliedmind.ai.

6. International Data Transfers

Our services are operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page or through direct notification where appropriate.
