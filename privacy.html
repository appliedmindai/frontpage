Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Applied Mind Company (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services, including our iOS applications and web platforms.

Data Sensitivity Notice Our applications utilize advanced data processing to analyze inputs such as images, audio, and text. While this analysis is conducted internally by Applied Mind, we recommend that users avoid capturing or sharing highly sensitive personal identifiers (such as government IDs or financial details) during active sessions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, secure, and improve our services. This includes:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and organization name.
  • User Content: Files, documents, and other structured content you explicitly upload.
  • Communications: Feedback, survey responses, and support requests.

B. Sensor & Input Data

To support core application functionality, we may request access to specific device sensors. We only access these sensors when you are actively using features that require them.

  • Camera & Visual Inputs: We access the camera to capture images or video frames. These inputs are processed to provide visual feedback, overlay information, or analyze the environment as requested by the user.
  • Microphone & Audio Inputs: We access the microphone to capture voice commands, memos, or audio queries for processing.

C. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Actions taken within the app, features used, and interaction duration.
  • Device & Log Data: IP address, device model, OS version, and crash logs.
  • Metadata: Technical data related to the files you upload (e.g., file size, timestamps, format).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following business purposes:

A. Service Delivery & Processing

  • Core Functionality: To process user inputs (visual, audio, or text) and generate relevant outputs or responses.
  • Interactive Features: To enable real-time feedback mechanisms within the application interface.

B. Quality Assurance & Internal Review

To ensure the accuracy and reliability of our systems, a subset of anonymized interactions may be reviewed by our internal engineering team. All reviewers are Applied Mind personnel or contracted specialists bound by strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs). This review process is strictly for product improvement and debugging purposes.

C. R&D and Security

  • System Improvement: To analyze performance metrics and refine our processing algorithms.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent activity, ensure compliance with our terms, and protect our platform.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted vendors (e.g., cloud infrastructure, processing providers) who handle data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or valid legal process.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security and Retention

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information.

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the requested services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.
  • Enforce our agreements.

Note on Processed Data: Inputs that have been anonymized for quality assurance purposes are disconnected from your user account and may be retained securely to prevent system regression.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal data.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and associated data. To request deletion, please contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" feature within the application settings.
  • Permissions: You can revoke Camera or Microphone access at any time in your device settings (this will disable related features).

Applied Mind does not sell your personal information. To formally record a "Do Not Sell" request, email us at privacy@appliedmind.ai.

6. International Data Transfers

Our services are operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page or through direct notification where appropriate.
